Who We Are

Science Is for Everyone

ASRO (Applied Science Research Organization) is a non-profit educational campaign conducted by the local youth and students of Ward No. 4, Biratnagar Metropolitan City. Our primary goal is to connect traditional classroom science with practical, hands-on modern science.

We operate with 9 core members and a skilled technical team of 14, all dedicated volunteers committed to making quality science education accessible to every student in our community.

Founded in Ward No. 4

ASRO was established by local youth passionate about bridging science education gaps.

Mobile Lab Launched

First mobile lab program delivered to partner schools at Rs. 55 per student.

VR Program Introduced

Virtual reality sessions integrated into our science education offerings.

Growing Community

Expanding team, partnerships, and student reach across Biratnagar in 2026.

Governance

Our Internal Niyamawali

The rules and constitution that guide how ASRO operates with integrity, transparency, and safety.

🏛️ Law Administration

A strict internal constitution (Niyamawali) has been prepared and formally approved by the Law Administrator. All members are bound by this document.

👑 Supreme Authority

Final decisions are made through collective consensus of the 9 core members. No single individual can override the group decision.

⚖️ Discipline & Expulsion

Members who breach organizational confidentiality or cause financial harm can be expelled (Kick out) at any time by core member vote.

🏠 Asset Security

Equipment purchased from ward or organizational funds becomes organizational property. Members assigned equipment are responsible for its safety.

🧪 Chemical Safety

Students must wear Gloves and Goggles during all chemical experiments. No chemicals can be handled without supervision from an ASRO member.

💰 Equipment Insurance

Expensive equipment like VR headsets — if broken by students while using — will be repaired from the organizational fund, not billed to the student.

📱 Digital Content Control

VR content must be strictly educational. Content is curated and controlled by the Secretary and Law Administrator — no unauthorized material allowed.

📢 Parental Advocacy

Parents who support their children's science journey are acknowledged and celebrated by the ward as key stakeholders in ASRO's mission.
