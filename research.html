Research & Programs

From mobile labs to VR classrooms — our science programs make research tangible and accessible for every student in Biratnagar.

Our Approach

Science That You Can Touch & Feel

At ASRO, we believe that the most powerful way to learn science is through direct experience. Our programs are designed to take students beyond textbooks and into real laboratory environments where they can observe, measure, experiment, and discover.

Every program is supervised by trained ASRO members and follows strict safety protocols. We work closely with schools, parents, and Ward No. 4 to deliver accessible, affordable, and impactful science education.

🚐 Mobile Lab Fee

Just Rs. 55 per student — our mobile lab visits your school and delivers full hands-on science experiments, making quality science accessible for everyone.

🛡️ Safety First

All experiments are conducted under ASRO member supervision with mandatory PPE — gloves and goggles for chemical experiments. Student safety is always our top priority.
