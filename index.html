Our Mission

Science For Every Student in Biratnagar

ASRO (Applied Science Research Organization) is a non-profit run by the youth of Ward No. 4. We believe every student deserves access to hands-on science education, regardless of their background.

🔬

Practical Learning

Students use real telescopes, microscopes, and lab equipment — learning by doing, not just reading.

🚐

Mobile Laboratory

Our travelling lab visits schools at just Rs. 55 per student, removing cost as a barrier to science.

🌟

Community Driven

Rooted in Ward No. 4, we collaborate with local offices, schools, and families to build a science culture.

What We Do

Programs & Services

From mobile labs to VR classrooms, ASRO delivers cutting-edge science education to students across Biratnagar.

🔭

Instrument Training

Hands-on sessions with telescopes, microscopes, and scientific instruments. Students learn how to operate, calibrate, and record findings.

🧪

Chemical Experiments

Supervised chemical reaction experiments with complete safety protocols — gloves, goggles, and ASRO member oversight at all times.

🚐

Mobile Lab Program

Science comes to schools. Our mobile lab travels to partner schools, delivering experiments for just Rs. 55 per student.

🥽

VR Science Experiences

Immersive virtual reality sessions for topics like atomic structure, planetary orbits, and biological systems — with strict content controls.

🌌

Astronomy Awareness

Sky-watching events, telescope nights, and astronomy workshops to spark curiosity about space science in young Nepali students.

🤝

Community Programs

Collaborating with Ward No. 4 on cultural events, science fairs, and community outreach to build a science-first culture locally.
