FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our STEM programs, IoT training, robotics workshops, and astronomy education.

Schools can partner with ASRO for STEM education programs including IoT training, robotics workshops, astronomy observation, and mobile lab visits. Contact us via the form or email to schedule hands-on science learning for your students.

ASRO's programs are designed to be affordable. Mobile lab sessions cost Rs. 55 per student. Some community astronomy events and science workshops are completely free. We believe cost should never be a barrier to STEM education.

We welcome passionate individuals who share our mission for STEM education in Nepal. Contact us via the form to join our team as a science educator, robotics mentor, IoT trainer, or astronomy guide. We seek science enthusiasts and educators.

Yes! As a non-profit, ASRO relies on community support to fund equipment, mobile lab operations, robotics kits, and IoT training programs. Please contact us to contribute to hands-on science education for Nepali students.

All ASRO experiments follow strict safety protocols. Chemical experiments require mandatory gloves and goggles. IoT training uses safe, low-voltage components. VR content is educational and curated. Robotics workshops have trained supervision. Student safety is our highest priority.
