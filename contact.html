Frequently Asked Questions

Schools can partner with ASRO by filling out our contact form above or reaching out directly to our Secretary, Prakash Thakur. We offer the mobile lab program for just Rs. 55 per student, bringing science experiments directly to your campus.

ASRO's programs are designed to be affordable. Mobile lab sessions cost Rs. 55 per student. Some community events are completely free. We believe cost should never be a barrier to science education.

We welcome passionate individuals who share our mission. Contact us via the form above or reach out to Councillor Sneha Pokharel through our Planning & Partnership channel. We look for science enthusiasts, educators, and skilled technical volunteers.

Yes! As a non-profit, ASRO relies on community support to fund equipment, mobile lab operations, and new programs. Please contact us with your interest in sponsorship and our Treasurer Deepshika Phuyal's team will connect with you.

All ASRO experiments are conducted under supervision of trained ASRO members. Chemical experiments require mandatory gloves and goggles. VR content is curated by our Secretary and Law Administrator. Student safety is always our highest priority.
